What is it ABOUT?

Firearms and Sharp Object Detection (FSOD) are system that we developed in order to provide real-time detection for many type of weapon, mainly firearms and sharp objects in order give preventative measure for possible crime occurrences. Real-time Detection

Our system have the the capability to provide real-time detection up to 80 categorized objects.

Push Notification

Our system will immediately sent push notification unto your phone or e-mail after threats are detected.

Our system will provide suggestions that you can do in order to improve your security and safeness.
